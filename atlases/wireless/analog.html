I envelope AM — Amplitude Modulation

s(t) = [1 + m·cos(2π f_m t)] · cos(2π f_c t)

Where it is used
OOK ASK QAM
02 / FM

I discrim. FM — Frequency Modulation

s(t) = cos( 2π f_c t + β·sin(2π f_m t) )

Where it is used

Where it is used
